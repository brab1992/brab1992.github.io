Links

Links. They're everywhere and represent everything. Below are some of the links that I frequently navigate to on the web:

  • The Onion: For a more creative, satirical publication I turn to The Onion. The Onion writes from an imaginative perspective that shows the other side to popular and more serious news stories being covered by bigger news outlets.
  • BuzzFeed: An interesting social news website that covers stories on pop culture, usually in list form using comical GIFs. Buzzfeed is also a popular website to take quizzes on. These quizzes can tell you where you want to live, what your favorite snack should be or even what celebrity you're most compatiable with.
  • The Independent Florida Alligator: UF's student newspaper. This news outlet keeps students and Gainesville residents up-to-date on happenings around the city and on UF's campus. Articles that I have written can be seen here, here and here.
  • Wordpress: Wordpress is an interesting social media website. Users can create their own website and make a blog to share to other people around the world. I have a blog through Wordpress, and you can see it here.
